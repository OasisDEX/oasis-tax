Token.tax a DappHub.com service

Compute capital gains and income from your blockchain activity

If you traded or earned any blockchain assets (such as ETH, REP, MKR, DGD, BTS, SDB, STEEM, or many others) on an Ethereum dapp or on Steem or BitShares, you likely had taxable events and may have a tax liability.

Token.tax is a service that parses a variety of decentralized apps and blockchains where tokens are earned and/or exchanged, and summarizes them in a way your accounting software can understand. It will collect all relevant data for an arbitrary number of trades and email it to you for just $1 — payable as SBD, bitUSD, or ETH.

Our data works well with https://cointracking.info because they have good price information (useful in obscure token-for-token trades), and because it allows you to aggregate trade data from centralized exchanges. This, combined with our income data (relevant for Steem users), should cover most crypto-asset-related tax issues.
